Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

TETRAX is a simple puzzle game. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you.

1. About This App

TETRAX is a block puzzle game developed by Tap Tap Droid and available on Android and Android Automotive OS. The app consists of a game interface displayed inside a WebView with optional advertising provided by Google AdMob.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Account credentials or registration data
  • Location data
  • Device contacts, photos, or files
  • Usage analytics or crash reports from our own systems

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

TETRAX saves your high scores locally on your device using the browser's localStorage API. This data never leaves your device and is only used to display your personal best scores within the game. You can clear it at any time by clearing the app's data from your device settings.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

TETRAX displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to show you relevant ads. This may include:

  • Your device's advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address (used to infer approximate location for ad targeting)
  • App interaction data (ad impressions, clicks)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by Tap Tap Droid. For full details on how Google uses this data, please review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time through your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (Android 12+) or Opt out of Ads Personalisation (earlier versions).

5. Children's Privacy

TETRAX is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided personal information through this app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

6. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used by TETRAX is Google AdMob. No other SDKs, analytics platforms, or data processors are integrated in this app.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
